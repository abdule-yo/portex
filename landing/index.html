How it works

1
Open a ChatGPT or Claude conversation and click the Portex icon.
2
Click "Save Current Chat" — the extension reads the page and stores the session locally.
3
Click "Summarize" to compress the session (optional: use your own AI API key for better quality).
4
Select a target provider and click "Inject" — Portex opens the tab and pastes the context for you.
